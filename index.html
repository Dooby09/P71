Campus Life


Junior School Hub

Was constructed in 2018.

The Junior School Hub is a 2-storey building constructed in 2018 consisting of 15 classrooms which are used to teach Year levels Kindagarten to Year 4. This magnificent building has a balcony and also an indoor slide which is used to entertain students.



SPACE Building

Was constructed in 2019.

The SPACE Building consists of a basketball court made from a one of a kind material in the southern hemisphere and a massive stage used for performences. There are three classes located on the mezzanine floor and an office area for the HPE staff.



Early Learning Center

Was constructed in 2004.

The Early Learning Center is a place of growth and nurturing at Brindabell Christian College. Children from the age of 3 yrs old can be enrolled for a daycare program where they will be takne care of by our staff.

Whats New

  • New School Canteen

    The food options and respnsibilities to consider, canteens must be better organised and more efficient than they have been in the past.   • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    This infroms parents about their child's/children's activities in the school instantly. Once a student reaches the school, the parents will be informed about the status via text messages. This helps schools keep a record of the students' absenteeism. Students can mark the attendance by logging in, and this is stored in a database, which can later be checked by the Admin. The tracker has so much potential that it can identify which students are irregular to their classes and not ready to establish connectivity with the actual concepts of thier curriculam's subjects. As soon as you enter a student's Enrollment Number or Name, details like availability in classes, number of lectures attended and will be displayed and no proxies are possible since the tracker is supported by cloud capabilities. Even the security offered can be so robust and powerful that those students who always fool their deans are caught in a shorter span- just after the admin enters login details into this tracker. All this will improve accuracy and transperancy in any of the educational institutions as students and their parents are pre-informed about the real time status of leave requests and absenteeism in a confidential manner and at reduced costs.
